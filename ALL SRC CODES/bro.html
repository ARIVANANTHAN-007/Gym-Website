Bro Workout Split

What is the Bro Workout Split?

The Bro workout split is a workout routine typically followed by individuals who focus primarily on building muscle size and achieving a muscular physique.

How does it work?

The Bro workout split typically involves training each muscle group once per week, allowing for ample recovery time. This split often emphasizes high volume and intensity, targeting individual muscle groups with multiple exercises.

Sample Bro Workout Split:

Chest Day:

  • Bench Press
  • Incline Dumbbell Press
  • Chest Flyes
  • Push-ups
  • Cable Crossovers

Back Day:

  • Deadlifts
  • Barbell Rows
  • Pull-ups
  • Lat Pulldowns
  • Dumbbell Rows

Leg Day:

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Leg Press
  • Leg Extensions
  • Hamstring Curls

Shoulder Day:

  • Overhead Press
  • Arnold Press
  • Lateral Raises
  • Rear Delt Flyes
  • Upright Rows

Arm Day:

  • Barbell Curls
  • Hammer Curls
  • Tricep Dips
  • Skull Crushers
  • Cable Pushdowns
