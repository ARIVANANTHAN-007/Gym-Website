Push Pull Legs Workout Split

What is the Push Pull Legs (PPL) Workout Split?

The Push Pull Legs (PPL) workout split is a popular training program that divides your workouts into three main categories: push exercises, pull exercises, and leg exercises.

How does it work?

In the PPL workout split, you typically train your push muscles (e.g., chest, shoulders, triceps) on one day, your pull muscles (e.g., back, biceps) on another day, and your leg muscles (e.g., quadriceps, hamstrings) on a separate day. This split allows you to target specific muscle groups more effectively and allows for sufficient recovery between workouts.

Sample PPL Workout Split:

Push Day:

  • Bench Press
  • Shoulder Press
  • Tricep Dips
  • Push-ups
  • Lateral Raises

Pull Day:

  • Deadlifts
  • Pull-ups
  • Barbell Rows
  • Bicep Curls
  • Hammer Curls

Leg Day:

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Leg Press
  • Leg Curls
  • Calf Raises
